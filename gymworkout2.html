Plank

A plank is an isometric core strength exercise that involves holding a position similar to a push-up. Planks can work your entire body, including your core, legs, back, arms, and shoulders, and can help build muscle strength and endurance. They can also help improve your balance and reduce your risk of back injury.

cable flies

Cable Flys, also known as Cable Chest Flys or Cable Crossovers, are a popular strength training exercise primarily targeting the chest muscles. Here's a brief overview: Muscles Targeted: Primary Muscle: Pectoralis Major (chest muscles). Secondary Muscles: Anterior Deltoids (front shoulder muscles), Triceps (back of the upper arm).
